Careers

Become a Team

Join Our Team and grow

We believe that joining our Mav team is an exciting opportunity for individuals looking to make a meaningful impact in the field of marketing, and we are looking for individuals who share our enthusiasm and commitment to delivering exceptional marketing solutions. Joining our team means becoming part of a dynamic and talented group of professionals dedicated to making a difference in the marketing industry. If you are ready to challenge yourself, grow professionally, and be a part of an exciting journey, we welcome you to join our team and contribute your skills and expertise to our collective success. Together, we can create impactful marketing campaigns that make a difference for our clients and their audiences.

Digital Marketing Specialist

You will be responsible for developing and executing digital marketing strategies across various channels such as SEO, PPC advertising, social media, content marketing, and email marketing. You will work closely with clients to understand their goals and objectives, analyze data to optimize campaigns, and drive measurable results.

Social Media Manager

You will be responsible for developing and implementing social media strategies for our clients. You will create engaging content, manage social media accounts, monitor performance, and interact with the audience to build brand awareness and drive engagement. Strong knowledge of various social media platforms and trends is essential for this role.

Graphic Designer

You will be responsible for creating visually appealing designs for various marketing materials such as brochures, banners, ads, infographics, and social media visuals. Proficiency in design software and a keen eye for aesthetics are essential for this role.

SEO Specialist

You will be responsible for optimizing websites to improve their search engine rankings and visibility. You will conduct keyword research, perform on-page and technical SEO optimizations, analyze data, and implement strategies to increase organic traffic and conversions.

Account Manager

You will be the main point of contact for our clients. You will build strong relationships, understand their business needs, and coordinate the execution of marketing campaigns. Strong communication and project management skills are essential for this role.
